## Part IV: The Joseph Narrative

- **Joseph's Dreams & Rejection** (Gen 37) <br> Jealous brothers sell Joseph to Ishmaelite traders.
- **Judah and Tamar** (Gen 38) <br> Judah's scandal; trickery; birth of Perez and Zerah. <br> **Note:** Preserves the Messianic line (Ancestor of David/Jesus).
- **Joseph and Potiphar** (Gen 39) <br> Joseph serves faithfully; falsely accused; imprisoned.
- **The Prisoner's Dreams** (Gen 40) <br> Interpretation for Cupbearer and Baker.
- **Pharaoh's Dreams** (Gen 41:1--40) <br> 7 years plenty/famine predicted.
- **Joseph in Power** (Gen 41:41--57) <br> Exalted to Governor; marriage; birth of Manasseh/Ephraim.
- **First Journey to Egypt** (Gen 42) <br> Brothers buy grain; Joseph tests them as spies.
- **Second Journey to Egypt** (Gen 43) <br> Return with Benjamin; Joseph feasts with them.
- **The Silver Cup Test** (Gen 44) <br> Benjamin framed; Judah offers his life as substitute.
- **Identity Revealed** (Gen 45) <br> Joseph reveals himself; family reconciliation.
- * **[List] Migration to Egypt** (Gen 46:1--27) <br> _Summary:_ List of 70 family members moving to Goshen. <br> _Future Context:_ Transition from nomadic family to nation-in-waiting.
- **Jacob Meets Pharaoh** (Gen 46:28--47:12) <br> Settlement in Goshen.
- **Joseph's Famine Policy** (Gen 47:13--31) <br> Acquisition of land for Pharaoh; Jacob requests Canaan burial.
- **Blessing the Sons** (Gen 48) <br> Jacob blesses Ephraim and Manasseh (younger over older).
- **Prophecy Over the Tribes** (Gen 49:1--28) <br> Jacob's final words to the 12 sons.
- **Death of Jacob** (Gen 49:29--50:14) <br> Burial in Machpelah.
- **Joseph Reassures Brothers** (Gen 50:15--21) <br> "You intended harm, God intended good."
- **Death of Joseph** (Gen 50:22--26) <br> Dies with promise of return to Canaan.
