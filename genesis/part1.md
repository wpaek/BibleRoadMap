## Part I: Primeval History (The Origins)

- **The Creation Week** (Gen 1:1--2:3) <br> Creation of the universe and life in six days; Sabbath rest on the seventh.
- **Adam and Eve in the Garden** (Gen 2:4--25) <br> Formation of man/woman, the Garden, and the institution of marriage.
- **The Fall of Man** (Gen 3:1--24) <br> Temptation by the serpent; entry of sin; curses and expulsion from Eden.
- **Cain and Abel** (Gen 4:1--16) <br> Jealousy and the first murder; Cain exiled to Nod.
- **[List] The Line of Cain** (Gen 4:17--22) <br> _Summary:_ First city-builders and inventors (herding, music, metallurgy). <br> _Future Context:_ Rise of human civilization independent of God.
- **Lamech's Song of Vengeance** (Gen 4:23--24) <br> Lamech boasts of killing a man and escalating violence 77-fold.
- **[List] The Line of Seth (Adam to Noah)** (Gen 4:25--32; Gen 5) <br> _Summary:_ The faithful lineage, including Enoch who walked with God. <br> _Future Context:_ Establishes the "Seed of the Woman" leading to Jesus (Luke 3).
- **The Wickedness of Man** (Gen 6:1--8) <br> Corruption peaks; "sons of God" marry "daughters of humans" (Nephilim).
- **The Great Flood** (Gen 6:9--8:19) <br> Noah builds the Ark; global judgment by water; waters recede.
- **The Noahic Covenant** (Gen 8:20--9:17) <br> God promises never to flood the earth again (Sign: Rainbow).
- **The Curse of Canaan** (Gen 9:18--29) <br> Noah's drunkenness; Ham's shameful act; curse placed on Canaan.
- **[List] The Table of Nations** (Gen 10) <br> _Summary:_ Clans of Japheth, Ham, and Shem populate the earth. <br> _Future Context:_ Origins of Israel's neighbors/enemies (Babylon, Egypt, Canaanites).
- **The Tower of Babel** (Gen 11:1--9) <br> Rebellion at Shinar; God confuses languages and scatters humanity.
- **[List] The Line of Shem to Abram** (Gen 11:10--32) <br> _Summary:_ Genealogy linking Noah's son Shem to Abram in Ur. <br> _Future Context:_ Connects world history to Israel's specific patriarch.
